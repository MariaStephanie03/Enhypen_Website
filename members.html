Lee Heeseung
이희승

Bambi 🐹

The center of Enhypen. He was born in Uiwang, South Korea on October 15th, 2001. He is also a former BigHit Entertainment trainee with TXT members. He ranked 5th in the final episode of I-LAND with 1,137,323 votes. “Let’s live diligently as life pass by”.

Park Sunghoon
박성훈

Ice Prince 🐧

He was born Namyangju, Gyeonggi, South Korea on December 8th, 2002. He is a former BigHit Entertainment trainee. Sunghoon ranked 6th on the final episode with 1,088,413 votes. He was a former figure skater and two-time national junior silver medalist who represented South Korea in various international competitions. He also has a dog named Gaeul. He’s currently an MC on Music Bank alongside IVE‘s Wonyoung. “Just do it!”.

Sim Jaeyun
심재윤

Aussie Boy 🐶

He was born in South Korea but raised in Australia on November 15th, 2002. He ranked 3rd on the final episode of I-LAND with 1,179,633 votes. Jake is a junior at Dwight School Seoul. Jake has a dog named Layla. He also ranked 45 on TC Candler’s Most Handsome Faces of 2020. “Live with positive vibes” and “work hard & play hard”.

Park Jongseong
박종성

Angry Bird 🦅

He was born in Seattle, Washington, U.S on April 20th, 2002. He ranked 2nd in the final episode of I-LAND with 1,182,889 votes. Jay trained at LP Dance Academy before joining BigHit. “Live your life the way you were born” and “Let’s just talk and live. I am human too! Can’t I even talk? I’m really mad. Anyways, you’ve done a great job”.

Yang Jungwon
양정원

Jung One 🐱

The leader of Enhypen. Born on February 9th, 2004. He ranked 1st in the final episode with 1,417,620 votes. He is a former SM Entertainment (2017-2018) and BigHit Entertainment (2018-2019) trainee. He has a pet dog named Maeumi, who is named after the dog in the drama Heart Is

Kim Sunoo
김선우

Aegyo Master 🦊

He was born in Suwon, Gyeonggi, South Korea on June 24th, 2003. He placed 8th in the final episode with 935,771 votes but was the producer’s choice to debut. Sunoo’s favorite colors are mint, purple, pink, and blue. He also loves mint chocolate. “To have passion all year round”.

Nishimura Riki
西村 力

Maknae Spoiler 🐆

He was born in Okayama, Japan on December 9th, 2005. His Korean name is Nishimura Chulsoo (니시무라 철수). Ni-ki was ranked 4th in the final episode with 1,140,718 votes. Ni-ki was in a group called SHINee Kids, where he was as Key through 2016-2017. He has danced with SHINee for multiple stages. He was named as I-LAND’s No.1 Dancer / I-LAND’s Best Dancer. “Dance is life”.

About Us

ENHYPEN (엔하이픈) consists of seven members of the survival show I-LAND under BE:LIFT Lab, namely Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki.
