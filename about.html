E N H Y P E N

One, Two, Connect!
Annyeonghaseyo, we are Enhypen.

ENHYPEN (엔하이픈) is the first boyband created by BELIFT LAB, a joint venture between HYBE and CJ E&M Entertainment (previously known as BigHit Entertainment),consisting of seven multinational members Heeseung, Jay, Jake, Sunghoon, Sunoo, Jungwon, and Ni-ki. They are the finalists of the global music competition show "I-Land", which finished airing in September 2020. The members have already shown infinite possibilities and growth not only as individuals, but also as a team. ENHYPEN is a mix of an en dash and a hyphen that connects different words to create new meaning, members of ENHYPEN will connect, discover each other, and grow together. ENHYPEN debuted on November 30th, 2020 with their mini-album “BORDER: DAY ONE” and title track “Given-Taken”
